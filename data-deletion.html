Data Deletion Instructions for Public Domain

If you wish to request the deletion of your personal data from Public Domain, please follow the steps below:

  1. Contact Information:
    • Call us at 13126002144 to request data deletion.
    • Email us at data-deletion@publicdomain.com.
  2. Provide Your User Information:

    In your message, please provide the following details:

    • Full name
    • Email address (if applicable)
    • Any other identifying details for verification
  3. Timeline for Deletion:

    Your data will be deleted within 30 days after we process your request.

  4. Confirmation:

    Once your request is processed, you will receive a confirmation of the data deletion.
